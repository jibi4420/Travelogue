Yelagiri is surrounded by lush forests and beautiful orchards, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers. The hill station comprises 14 small villages and is spread across an area of about 30 square kilometers. The climate in Yelagiri remains pleasant throughout the year, with summers being mild and winters being cool. The monsoon season brings moderate rainfall, adding to the scenic beauty of the place.Yelagiri offers several attractions that cater to different types of travelers. Maldives is a stunning island nation located in the Indian Ocean, southwest of Sri Lanka and India. Known for its crystal-clear waters, white sandy beaches, and luxurious resorts, the Maldives is a dream destination for travelers seeking relaxation and natural beauty.Maldives consists of around 1,190 coral islands grouped into 26 atolls. These islands are spread over approximately 90,000 square kilometers, making the Maldives one of the most geographically dispersed countries in the world. The nation experiences a tropical climate, with warm temperatures throughout the year and two distinct monsoon seasons.Tourism is the backbone of the Maldivian economy. The country's beautiful resorts, underwater villas, and thrilling water sports attract millions of visitors annually. Activities such as snorkeling, scuba diving, and dolphin watching are popular among tourists. Fishing is another major industry, contributing significantly to the economy.The Maldives is a paradise on Earth, offering breathtaking natural beauty and a unique island lifestyle. Despite facing environmental challenges, it remains a top tourist destination and a symbol of tropical luxury. Whether for adventure, relaxation, or cultural experiences, the Maldives continues to captivate visitors from around the world.
